Focaccia

A focaccia is an italian flatbread usually finished with simple toppings

This recipe is like this, like this and that. It is traditionally made with ingredients and eaten by people in Italy. It is a great snack to enjoy with some wine or beer and you can even make sandwiches.

  • 500g white flour
  • 350g water
  • 10g salt
  • 65g levain
  • 15g olive oil
  • Toppings of choice

Instructions:

  1. Put the flour in a bowl and mix in the salt
  2. Add the water
  3. Add the levain
  4. Mix until there are no dry bits left
  5. Let it rest for 20-30 min
  6. Add 25g of water and fold the dough into itself
  7. Wait for 20-30 min, add the olive oil and fold again
  8. Do folds spaced 20-30 min in between until the dough is smooth and elastic. A total of 3-5 folding steps should be enough (but don't add any more water or olive oil in the further steps)
  9. Wait until the dough rises
  10. Spread it into a baking tray (oil-coated), add the toppings and some extra olive oil, pressing down the dough to make the olive oil sink into little pools
  11. Bake that shit at 230°C-250°C for around 30 min or until you feel you achieved desired color
  12. Enjoy
